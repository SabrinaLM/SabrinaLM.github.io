The Move to Aberdeen

Advice from Current Students on how to handle the move to university

The unofficial guide to life at the University of Aberdeen. The move to University, what to do in the first few weeks and thereafter. The transition to living and studying independently away from home, and often in a different country can be overwhelming as well as exciting. Here we provide some guidance and a few tips to make the move slightly easier.
